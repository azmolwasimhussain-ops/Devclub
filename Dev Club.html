About Marvel

The House of Ideas

Marvel Comics is an American comic book publisher and the flagship property of Marvel Entertainment, a division of The Walt Disney Company since 2009.

Founded in 1939 as Timely Comics by Martin Goodman, the company has grown from a humble publishing house to a multimedia giant.

Marvel has created thousands of superhero characters, including Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Black Panther.

These characters have not only dominated the comic book industry but have also become global icons through blockbuster movies, TV shows, and merchandise.

Marvel Timeline

1939

Timely Comics publishes Marvel Comics #1, introducing the Human Torch and Namor the Sub-Mariner.

1961

The Fantastic Four debut, marking the beginning of the Marvel Universe as we know it.
